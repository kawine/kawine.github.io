I'll be joining Stanford NLP in the fall as a PhD student. Until recently, I was a BMO National Scholar and John H. Moss Scholar at the University of Toronto, where I worked with Graeme Hirst and David Duvenaud.

My research interests are in representation learning for NLP (e.g., word embeddings, additive compositionality) and ML fairness. While at Google, I also worked on graph representation learning and information extraction.
